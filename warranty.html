Perfume Guarantee Exchange Service

What is Perfume Guarantee?

Finding the right perfume isn't always easy. That’s why we’ve created Perfume Guarantee, the fuss-free way to exchange your perfume if you're not completely satisfied with your online purchase.

What are the conditions for exchange?

Exchange is subject to safe return of goods. Please ensure products are packed properly and returned in original condition with packaging.

What products are excluded?

Sets, travel sizes, home fragrances, bath & body products and promotional items are not eligible for exchange.

Where should I send my return?

Please submit your Perfume Guarantee Form and our team will contact you with complete return instructions.

Can I exchange for a perfume of different price?

Yes! You can exchange for a fragrance of equal or higher value. If there is a balance amount, we will inform you by email.

Can I keep my Perfume Guarantee sample?

Yes! Your Perfume Guarantee sample is complimentary and you may keep it.

Is there a cost to return?

Return shipping charges are applicable and depend on your delivery location.

Can I get more samples?

Additional samples may be included depending on availability.
